How x402 Pricing Works

OMA-AI uses the x402 payment protocol enable instantmicropayments for API calls. Instead of locking yourself into monthly subscriptions, you pay per use with USDC on Base or Solana networks. Payments settle instantly, and publishers receive 90% of every transaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I exceed my daily limit?

On the Free tier, you'll receive HTTP 429 responses. Upgrading to Pro removes all rate limits with generous daily quotas.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription?

Yes, you can downgrade at any time. There are no contracts or cancellation fees. Your data remains accessible.

How do I become a publisher?

Visit /publish to list your API. You'll earn 90% revenue share on every call.
