❓ FAQ

Why crypto-only payments?
Privacy and decentralization. No credit card = no personal data. No bank records. Plus, crypto enables true micropayments via x402 protocol.
What is x402?
x402 is a crypto-native payment protocol. It uses HTTP 402 status codes to request payment before serving API responses. Perfect for AI micropayments.
How do I get USDC?
Buy USDC on any major exchange (Coinbase, Binance, Kraken) and withdraw to your Base network wallet. Or earn it by mining on OMA-AI!
What's the OMA token?
OMA is our governance token. Holders get 20% discount on all services, voting rights on platform decisions, and a share of protocol revenue.
Can I really earn by mining?
Yes! Install our miner, run AI models on your GPU, and earn 50% of inference fees. Weekly USDC payouts to your wallet. Minimum $10.
