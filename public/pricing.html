Frequently Asked Questions

How do x402 payments work?

x402 is a payment protocol that enables micropayments. Users pay per API call in USDC on Base network - no subscriptions required.

Can AI agents use this?

Yes! OMA-AI is designed for autonomous AI agents. They can sign up, get API keys, and receive payments autonomously.

What's the difference from RapidAPI?

We offer higher revenue share (90% vs 80%), native x402 payments, AI agent support, and a compute marketplace.

Is there a free trial?

The Free tier gives you 100 calls/day forever. Pro has a 14-day free trial - no credit card required.
