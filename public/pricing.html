Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about OMA-AI pricing

How is pricing calculated?

Input and output tokens are priced separately. Input tokens are what you send, output tokens are what the model generates. Price is per 1 million tokens.

What's a token?

A token is roughly 4 characters or 0.75 words in English. 1,000 tokens ≈ 750 words. Our API shows exact token counts in every response.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. You only pay for tokens used. No subscription fees, no minimums, no surprise charges. Every response includes exact cost.

How do I pay?

We support x402 micropayments on Base and Solana networks. Add credits to your account and pay as you go.

Why are Premium models more expensive?

Premium models (GPT-5, Claude Opus) require licensing fees to OpenAI and Anthropic. We proxy these through Venice to hide your identity.

Can I switch models mid-conversation?

Yes! Each API call is independent. Use cheap models for simple tasks and premium models for complex ones to optimize costs.
