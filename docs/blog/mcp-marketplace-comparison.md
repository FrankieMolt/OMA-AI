# MCP Marketplaces Compared: Smithery vs OMA-AI vs Direct

You have built an MCP. Now the question: where do you publish it?

## The Three Options

### 1. Direct (Your Own Website)
You host the MCP, handle auth, billing, and marketing yourself. Full control but zero discoverability.

### 2. Smithery.ai
A managed MCP marketplace with SDK integration. Built-in user base but 40% revenue share for paid MCPs.

### 3. OMA-AI
Open MCP marketplace with x402 micropayments. 90% revenue share for publishers. Gasless payments built in.

## Quick Comparison Table

| | Direct | Smithery | OMA-AI |
|--|-------|---------|--------|
| Platform fee | 0% | 40% | 5% |
| Setup complexity | High | Medium | Low |
| Payment handling | DIY | Built-in | Built-in (x402) |
| Discoverability | None | Medium | Growing |
| Self-hostable | N/A | No | Yes |

## The x402 Advantage

OMA-AI's x402 micropayments change the economics. Traditional subscription models mean you either overpay for rarely-used MCPs or publishers under-earn. With x402, pay exactly what you use at sub-cent resolution.

For AI agents making 10,000 calls per day to various MCPs, micropayments save hundreds per month vs subscription models.

## Recommendation

For most developers: publish to OMA-AI for the low friction and revenue share, also list on Smithery for additional discoverability. The platforms are not mutually exclusive — many publishers multi-list.
