### 2. Payment Systems

#### OMA-AI: x402 Gasless Payments ✅

**How it Works:**
1. User signs payment intent (off-chain, no gas)
2. OMA-AI relayer executes transaction (pays gas)
3. User pays $0 in gas
4. Developer receives 95% of call price

**Benefits:**
- **Zero gas fees** for users
- **Instant experience**
- **No gas token requirement**
- **Predictable costs**

**Supported Networks:**
- **Base** (USDC)
- **Solana** (USDC)

#### Smithery.ai: Unknown

No public information on gasless payments. Likely requires users to pay gas for transactions.

#### RapidAPI: Traditional Payments

Uses traditional payment processing with fees passed to users. No gasless payment system.
