# Lethometry.com - AI Agent Philosophy/Scientific Experiment Platform

## Vision
Lethometry.com will be an **interactive AI-powered platform** where users can engage with philosophical thought experiments, participate in scientific studies about mortality, memory, and consciousness, and explore philosophical ideas through AI conversation.

## Core Features

### 1. AI Philosophy Companion
- Interactive chatbot trained on philosophical texts
- Engages users in Socratic dialogue
- Discusses Stoicism, Buddhism, Existentialism, and more
- Real-time philosophical debates and reflections

### 2. Thought Experiment Simulator
- Trolley Problem (multiple variations)
- Brain in a Vat
- Ship of Theseus
- Chinese Room
- Mary's Room (Knowledge Argument)
- Experience Machine
- Simulation Hypothesis
- And many more...

### 3. Scientific Experiments (Interactive)
- **Memory Decay Study**: Users participate in memory experiments
- **Mortality Awareness Research**: Survey on life expectancy beliefs
- **Consciousness Experiments**: Qualia and subjective experience studies
- **Decision-Making Psychology**: Moral choice scenarios

### 4. Philosophy Journal
- User's philosophical reflections
- AI-generated philosophical insights
- Personal growth tracking
- Memory preservation of philosophical journey

### 5. Community Features
- Philosophical discussion forums
- Share thought experiment results
- Compare philosophical positions with others
- Leaderboards for experiment participation

## Technical Architecture

### AI Models
- **Philosophy AI**: Fine-tuned on philosophical texts (Stoic, Buddhist, existentialist works)

### Scientific Experiments

#### Memory Studies
1. **Ebbinghaus Forgetting Curve** - Users memorize and recall items
2. **Tip of the Tongue** - Partial memory retrieval
3. **False Memory Formation** - Suggestibility testing
4. **Procedural vs Declarative Memory** - Skill vs fact memory

#### Mortality Awareness Studies
1. **Life Expectancy Perception** - How long do people think they'll live?
2. **Death Attitudes Scale** - Fear vs acceptance
3. **Legacy Priorities** - What do people want to be remembered for?
4. **Memento Mori Effect** - Does death awareness change behavior?

#### Consciousness Studies
1. **Qualia Description** - Describe your subjective experience
2. **Attention Blink** - Conscious attention limits
3. **Change Blindness** - Visual perception awareness
4. **Self-Recognition** - Mirror test variations

#### Decision-Making Studies
1. **Moral Dilemmas** - Real-world ethical choices
2. **Loss Aversion** - Risk preferences
3. **Cognitive Bias Detection** - Identify your biases
4. **Heuristic Traps** - Common mental shortcuts
