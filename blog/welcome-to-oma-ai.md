# Welcome to OMA-AI: The Premier API Marketplace for AI Agents

We're thrilled to announce the launch of **OMA-AI (OpenMarketAccess)**, the premier API marketplace designed specifically for AI agents and MCP servers.

## 🎯 Our Mission

OMA-AI exists to accelerate the arrival of **Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)** by providing autonomous AI agents with the infrastructure they need to:

### 1. Discover APIs and MCP Servers
Agents can autonomously discover tools and services that match their requirements. From large language models to specialized APIs, find what you need to accomplish tasks.

### 2. Pay for Services Automatically
Our **x402 payment protocol** enables seamless, autonomous commerce. No human intervention required - agents can discover, negotiate, and pay for services on their own.

### 3. Perform Real-World Tasks
Access tools that enable agents to perform meaningful work:
- Scientific research and analysis
- Environmental protection monitoring
- Financial data processing
- Creative content generation
- And much more...

## 🚀 What Makes OMA-AI Different?

### For Both Humans AND Agents
OMA-AI isn't just for humans to browse. It's built from the ground up for **both human developers and autonomous AI agents**. Same APIs, same infrastructure, same payment system.

### Autonomous Commerce
The x402 protocol allows agents to:
- Discover services via APIs
- Execute transactions automatically
- Manage their own budgets
- Build reputation over time

### Open & Decentralized
Built on open standards with **USDC on Base** for fast, low-cost payments. No gatekeepers, no intermediaries.

## 📦 Getting Started

### For Developers
1. **List your API** - Add your service to the marketplace
2. **Set your price** - Define your per-use pricing
3. **Integrate x402** - Enable automatic payments
4. **Start earning** - Agents discover and pay you automatically

### For AI Agents
1. **Discover APIs** - Query the marketplace programmatically
2. **Filter by capability** - Find services that match your needs
3. **Use x402** - Pay automatically from your wallet
4. **Build reputation** - Establish trust for future transactions
